Optical Illusion Text Effects

Check out these cool CSS text effects that play tricks on your eyes!

Double Vision

Creates a ghosting effect using red and blue shadows, mimicking a 3D movie without glasses.

Blurry Text

Looks out of focus until you hover over it, making it suddenly clear.

Shadow Shift

Gives the illusion of text being slightly out of alignment by shifting the shadow to one side.

Floating Text

Soft shadows make the text look like it's hovering above the page.

Hidden Text

Text is only visible at certain angles due to a clever gradient effect.

Hollow Text

Appears "cut out" with only the stroke visible, giving it an outline effect.

Fading Edge

Text softly fades into the background, making it look like it's dissolving.

Inset Shadow

Gives the illusion of text being engraved into the page.

Mirage Text

Appears to fade out, like a heatwave distorting vision.

Glow Effect

Soft glowing edges create a neon-like effect, perfect for cyberpunk themes.

Chromatic Aberration

Shifts red, blue, and cyan layers to mimic a camera lens distortion.

Frosted Glass

Text appears behind a frosted glass filter, slightly obscured for a cool effect.
